Context

  • Joined late in production to formalize monetization and test strategy.
  • Requirement: lore-respectful systems and offers for Warhammer 40K.
  • Constraint: maintain fairness perception; avoid P2W escalation.

Goals

  • Populate launch shop with coherent value ladders and bundles.
  • Define first- and second-payment journeys with clear triggers.
  • Ship A/B framework for price points, bundles, and sales rhythms.

Approach

  • Adapt Supremacy 1914/WW3 learnings to reduce early-game friction.
  • Offer architecture: premium currency, subscription, faction-themed units.
  • Starter Pack ladder: timed entry offer → follow-up conversion → ramp.
  • Sales calendar: anchor beats, decoy tiers, and event-aligned bundles.

Responsibilities

  • Monetization strategy and A/B test design.
  • Entry sales strategy for tiered pay-class progression.
  • Coordinate lore-loyal monetization with design and licensors.
  • Analytics review and iteration planning.
