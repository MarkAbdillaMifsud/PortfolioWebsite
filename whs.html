Warhammer 40K: Supremacy — Monetization & Launch

Established a comprehensive monetization strategy for the launch of the title, based on best practices and lessons learned from other titles forming part of the Supremacy Franchise. This also involved establishing an A/B testing framework to gather data on user behaviour, preferred price points and ingame engagement.

Context

  • Joined the team late in production to flesh out monetization processes and strategic approach.
  • Lack of a cohesive plan on how to monetize the title while balancing this with fun game design.

Goals

  • Ensure population of shop with adequate offers and bundles.
  • Establish a clear flow for player first-time pay conversion.
  • Assist Game Design Team with ensuring offers and events are monetizable and in keeping with Warhammer lore.

My Approach

  • Utilise Supremacy 1914 monetization approach to make entry curve less daunting.
  • Provide adeqaute opportunities for players to monetize through Premium Currency, Subscriptions and Faction Units.
  • Establish a series of Starter Packs, aimed at ensuring adequate first and second payment flows.
  • Cohesive Sales Strategy to ensure better monetization on event days.

Responsibilities

  • Monetization strategy and A/B test design.
  • Entry point sales strategy to allow tiered payclass progression.
  • Coordinate lore loyal systems and approaches for monetization.
  • Data Analysis and adopting findings to iterate on designs.

System Designs

Shop Design

  • Introduced a launch shop for mobile and desktop.
  • Establish a Starter Pack and follow-up Faction Packs.
  • Drove DTC store strategy to ensure adequate revenue share subject to less fees.
