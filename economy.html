Machinations Crafting Economy

A loot→crafting economy prototyped in Machinations, then extended with gold sinks/sources, rarity & quality tiers, risk-based crafting, efficiency upgrades, inventory caps, and a dynamic market that reacts to stockpiling.

Base Loop

  • Loot source feeds a rarity gate into Common and Rare pools.
  • Craft consumes 3× Common + 1× Rare to create an Item.
  • With gold added: loot also yields d6 gold; craft costs 20 gold; selling an item returns 15 gold. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Early Balance Checks

  • 20-step prediction: median Items=16, Gold=19.
  • 100-step prediction: median Items=17, Gold=14. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Difficulty & Depth Additions

Legendary Drop (Luck)

  • Legendary pool at 5%; Rare adjusted to 35%.
  • Special craft: 3× Rare + 1× Legendary + 35 gold; sell for 25 gold. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Efficiency Upgrade (Progression)

  • Spend 40 gold per level (max 2).
  • Each level: −1 Common requirement; −5 gold craft cost (minimum 1 Common required). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Player Choice: Risk Craft

  • Alternative craft: 2× Common + 1× Rare + 15 gold with 50% success.
  • Standard craft increased to 5× Common + 2× Rare to justify risk. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Item Quality Tiers

  • Craft outcomes: 60% Common, 35% Rare, 15% Legendary.
  • Sell values: 15 / 25 / 50 gold respectively. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

Overproduction Penalties

  • Caps: Common 5, Rare 10, Gold 50.
  • Drains: −1 Common, −1 Rare, −25 Gold when caps are exceeded. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Dynamic Market Economy

  • Dynamic Loot: gold from loot scales as d6*(20/(Gold+20)) → diminishing returns with stockpiling.
  • CommonValue / RareValue: required inputs rise with pool size (min 2C/1R).
  • MarketPrice: selling price decreases as crafted supply grows. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Simulation Notes

  • 100-step run (manual sell): means — Items 4.96 (max 8), Common 12.13 (max 18), Rare 5.65 (max 9), Gold 11.36 (max 21).
  • Auto-sell enabled: Gold mean rises to 38.35 (max 56). :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
