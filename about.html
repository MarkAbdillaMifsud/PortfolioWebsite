About

I design sustainable economies and disciplined live operations for free-to-play strategy games. My work focuses on measurable impact: payer conversion, direct-to-consumer revenue share, and retention via systems and events players respect. I partner with UX/UI, product, data, and community to ship changes that move KPIs without resorting to pay-to-win.

Recent outcomes include a +37 percentage-point shift to DTC share through a webshop redesign, a +5% lift in first-time payer conversion via Starter Pack testing, and a 5× increase in alliance participation by structuring competitive play.

I value clarity, reproducible processes, and readable artifacts others can run with.
