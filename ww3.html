Supremacy: WW3 — Monetization & Live Ops

Redesigned the DTC webshop and seasonal live ops to shift revenue mix and improve early payer conversion. Delivered +37 percentage points DTC revenue share, +5% payer conversion, and 5× monthly alliance participation growth.

Context

  • Legacy webshop with weak visual hierarchy and low perceived value.
  • Season structure inconsistent across the year; low narrative cohesion.
  • Alliance competitions underutilized as a retention driver.

Goals

  • Shift revenue mix to DTC channels.
  • Improve first‑time payer conversion.
  • Make seasons repeatable, legible, and monetizable without P2W.

My Approach

  • Webshop redesign with bundle framing, decoy tiers, and clearer value ladders.
  • Season “rails”: elite units, flagship levels, missions, narrative beats.
  • Starter Pack experiments with price points, contents, and expiry windows.
  • Live‑ops schedule aligned to player lifecycle cohorts and LTV segments.

Responsibilities

  • Monetization strategy and A/B test design.
  • Collaboration with UX/UI on shop UX and visual systems.
  • Event economy and reward tuning; sink/source audits.
  • Stakeholder reporting and KPI tracking.

System Designs

Webshop Redesign

  • Introduced anchor/decoy pricing and clear tier naming.
  • Improved art direction and scarcity cues without dark patterns.
  • Localized psychological price points; surfaced subscription value.

Season Design

  • Repeatable template with elite units + mission arcs.
  • Flagship upgrades linked to season identity.
  • Clear earn/spend loops to avoid pay‑to‑win perception.

Gallery

Impact

  • +37pp increase in DTC revenue share following webshop redesign.
  • +5% increase in first‑time payer conversion via Starter Pack tests.
  • 5× increase in monthly alliance participation after competition revamp.

What I’d Tackle Next

  • Deeper cohort‑based offer personalization beyond age/LTV matrix.
  • Automated event ops dashboard with anomaly alerts.
  • Creative testing pipeline for shop art and copy.
